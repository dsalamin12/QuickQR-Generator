About QuickQR

Last updated: July 2026

Our Mission

QuickQR was created to provide a fast, free, and privacy-first QR Code Generator without forcing users to sign up or create accounts. We believe data security should be standard across utility applications.

Privacy First

Unlike conventional tools, your QR configurations are handled safely through client-side technology. All codes generate instantly inside your browser viewport, meaning your sensitive structural content never uploads to external cloud databases.

Features

  • URL QR Codes
  • WiFi QR Codes
  • WhatsApp QR Codes
  • vCard QR Codes
  • Email QR Codes
  • SMS QR Codes
  • PNG & SVG Downloads
  • Bulk QR Generation
  • Logo & Color Customization

Our Goal

We continuously expand QuickQR by introducing advanced tool capabilities, high-intent technical documentation blueprints, and operational updates while keeping the primary generator interface free and private for worldwide users.
