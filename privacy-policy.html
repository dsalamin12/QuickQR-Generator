Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

QuickQR respects your privacy. Our website is designed to generate QR codes directly inside your browser. We believe privacy should be the default—not an optional feature.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

QuickQR does not collect, upload, store or process:

  • Your QR code content
  • URLs you generate
  • Wi-Fi passwords
  • Phone numbers
  • Email addresses
  • vCards
  • SMS messages
  • Uploaded images or logos

Everything is generated locally inside your browser. Your data never leaves your device.

3. Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand general website usage, such as:

  • Number of visitors
  • Pages visited
  • Device types
  • Country-level statistics
  • General traffic sources

Google Analytics does not receive the contents of the QR codes you create.

4. Cookies

QuickQR may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and to remember your cookie consent preferences. You may refuse analytics cookies through our consent banner.

5. Third-Party Services

QuickQR currently uses:

  • Google Analytics

Additional services may be added in the future to improve the website.

6. Affiliate Links

In the future, some pages may contain affiliate links. If you purchase through those links, QuickQR may receive a small commission at no additional cost to you.

7. Children's Privacy

QuickQR is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

8. Security

Because QR codes are generated entirely inside your browser, your information is never transmitted to our servers. This significantly reduces privacy risks.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will always be available on this page.
