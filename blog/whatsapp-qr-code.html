How to Make a WhatsApp QR Code — Free, Fast & No Sign-Up

QuickQR Guide  ·  Free  ·  5 min read

WhatsApp is used by over 2 billion people worldwide. If you run a business, freelance, or just want people to reach you easily, a WhatsApp QR code is one of the smartest things you can add to your website, business card, or shop window.

Instead of someone having to save your number, open WhatsApp, find you, and start a chat — they simply scan your QR code and they're talking to you in seconds.

Who Should Use a WhatsApp QR Code?

  • Small business owners — let customers contact you instantly
  • Freelancers — on portfolios, resumes, and LinkedIn profiles
  • Restaurants & shops — for enquiries and orders
  • Real estate agents — on property listings and signage
  • Event organisers — on posters and event programmes
  • Tutors & coaches — on promotional materials

How to Create a WhatsApp QR Code — Step by Step

Step 1: Open QuickQR

Go to QuickQR — Free QR Code Generator. No account needed. No personal data collected. Works entirely in your browser.

Step 2: Click the WhatsApp Tab

In the content type tabs, select 💬 WhatsApp.

Step 3: Enter Your Phone Number

Select your country code (🇧🇩 +880 Bangladesh, 🇺🇸 +1 USA, 🇬🇧 +44 UK, 🇦🇪 +971 UAE etc.) and enter your WhatsApp phone number.

Step 4: Add a Pre-Filled Message (Recommended)

In the "Pre-filled Message" field, type a short opening message. For example:

  • "Hi, I'd like to know more about your services"
  • "Hello, I saw your QR code and want to place an order"

This removes friction — customers don't have to think of what to write. They just hit send.

Step 5: Customise & Download

Personalise your QR code's colour, shape, and optionally add your logo. Then click Download PNG (digital use) or Download SVG (printing).

Tips for a Better WhatsApp QR Code

  • Always include a pre-filled message — lowers the barrier and increases the number of people who actually message you
  • Add your logo — makes it look professional and branded
  • Test it yourself — before printing, scan with your own phone to confirm it works

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the person scanning need to have my number saved?

No. The QR code opens a WhatsApp chat directly — no contact saving needed on either side.

Does the QR code expire?

No. QuickQR generates static QR codes with no expiry date. Your WhatsApp QR code works permanently as long as your number stays the same.

Is my phone number stored anywhere?

No. QuickQR runs entirely in your browser. Your number is embedded in the QR code pattern itself and is never sent to any server.
