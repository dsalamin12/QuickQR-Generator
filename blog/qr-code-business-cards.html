QR Code for Business Cards — The Complete Guide (2026)

QuickQR Guide  ·  Free  ·  5 min read

Business cards have been around for centuries. But watching someone slowly type your number into their phone after a meeting is an experience that rarely ends in success — numbers get mistyped, cards get lost.

A QR code on your business card changes this entirely. One scan and all your contact details — name, phone, email, company, job title — are saved to their phone contacts automatically. No typing. No errors.

What Is a vCard QR Code?

A vCard QR code stores contact information in a standard format (called vCard or VCF). When someone scans it, their phone shows a prompt: "Add to Contacts?" — they tap Yes and your complete profile is saved instantly.

Why Add a QR Code to Your Business Card?

  • Saves a contact in 3 seconds instead of 3 minutes
  • No risk of typing errors in your phone number or email
  • Works even if they lose the physical card — they already saved you
  • Looks modern and tech-forward
  • Completely free to create

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the person scanning need an app?

No. The built-in camera app on all modern iPhones and Android phones can scan QR codes directly.

What if I change my phone number?

Generate a new QR code with updated details. This is why printing in small batches first is wise.
