## How to Find Your Instagram Profile Link

Before creating the QR code, you need your Instagram profile URL. It's always in this format:

```
https://www.instagram.com/yourusername
```

For example, if your Instagram handle is `@quickqr_official`, your link would be:
```
https://www.instagram.com/quickqr_official
```

Just replace `yourusername` with your actual Instagram handle. You don't need to log in anywhere to get this — it's public.

## Tips for Maximum Follower Conversions

**Always add a call to action.** The QR code alone doesn't tell people what to do. Add a short line above or below it:
- *"Scan to follow us on Instagram"*
- *"Follow for daily updates"*
- *"See our full portfolio — scan here"*

**Tell people what they'll get.** "Follow for weekly recipes" is more compelling than just "Follow us." Give them a reason.

**Use it at the right moment.** The best time to ask someone to follow you is right after a positive experience — after they've enjoyed your food, received your product, or attended your event. That's when their interest is highest.

**Make it big enough.** On a printed flyer, your QR code should be at least 3cm × 3cm. On a banner, larger still. Too small and people won't bother trying.

**Test it yourself.** Before printing 500 flyers, scan the QR code with your own phone and confirm it takes you to the right Instagram profile.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the person scanning need an Instagram account to view my profile?**
No. Instagram profiles are publicly visible even to people without accounts. They can view your posts from the browser. To follow you, they do need an Instagram account.

**Does the QR code expire?**
No. QuickQR creates static QR codes with no expiry date. Your Instagram QR code will work forever as long as your username stays the same.

**What if I change my Instagram username?**
If you change your handle, you'll need to generate a new QR code with the updated link. This is why it's worth finalising your username before printing materials in bulk.

**Can I use this for a Facebook or TikTok page too?**
Yes — exactly the same process. Just paste your Facebook or TikTok profile URL into the URL tab instead of your Instagram link.

**Can I add this to my Instagram bio or posts?**
Instagram doesn't display images in your bio, so you can't put it there directly. But you can share the QR code image as a post or Story with a caption like *"Save this to share my profile with friends."*
