QR Code for Instagram — Grow Your Followers with One Scan

QuickQR Guide  ·  Free  ·  5 min read

Getting people to follow your Instagram in real life is hard. You say your username, they try to find it, they misspell it, they give up. A QR code for your Instagram removes all of this — display it anywhere and anyone who scans it lands directly on your profile and can follow you in one tap.

Who Should Have an Instagram QR Code?

  • Small businesses — turn every in-person touchpoint into a follower opportunity
  • Restaurants and cafés — on tables, receipts, and packaging
  • Freelancers and creators — on business cards and portfolios
  • Market stall owners — on banners and packaging
  • Tutors, coaches, trainers — on leaflets and welcome packs

Find Your Instagram Profile Link

Your Instagram URL is always in this format:

https://www.instagram.com/yourusername

Replace yourusername with your actual Instagram handle. That's it — no login needed.

How to Create Your Instagram QR Code — Step by Step

Step 1: Open QuickQR

Visit QuickQR. No account needed.

Step 2: Select URL Tab

Click the 🔗 URL tab and paste your Instagram profile link.

Step 3: Add Instagram Branding

  • Colour: Use warm orange-to-pink or purple gradient to match Instagram's style
  • Logo: Upload your profile photo or Instagram icon as the centre logo
  • Dot style: "Rounded" or "Dots" for a softer, social-media feel

Step 4: Download

  • PNG — for digital sharing (Stories, posts, email, website)
  • SVG — for printing on flyers, banners, or business cards

Tips for Maximum Followers

  • Always add a call to action: "Scan to follow us on Instagram"
  • Tell people what they'll get: "Follow for weekly recipes" beats "Follow us"
  • Use it at the right moment: Right after a positive experience is when people are most likely to follow
  • Minimum size for print: 3cm × 3cm — smaller and people won't bother

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the person need an Instagram account to view my profile?

No. Instagram profiles are publicly visible even without an account. To follow you, they do need an account.

Does the QR code expire?

No. QuickQR creates static QR codes with no expiry date. Works forever as long as your username stays the same.

Can I use this for Facebook or TikTok too?

Yes — exactly the same process. Just paste your Facebook or TikTok profile URL instead.
