## What Is a WhatsApp QR Code?

A WhatsApp QR code is a QR code that, when scanned, opens a WhatsApp chat with your phone number directly — with an optional pre-written message already filled in.

When someone scans it:
1. WhatsApp opens on their phone automatically
2. Your chat window appears — ready to send
3. If you added a pre-filled message, it appears in the text box for them

No need to save your number. No searching. One scan and they're in.
